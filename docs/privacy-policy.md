# Privacy Policy — Waiheke Pets Alert

**Effective date:** 20 May 2026  
**Jurisdiction:** New Zealand  

**Important:** This is a draft. Have it reviewed for compliance with the **Privacy Act 2020 (NZ)** and other applicable obligations.

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy explains how **Waiheke Pets Alert** (“**we**”) collects, uses, stores, and shares **personal information** when you use our website or related services.

---

## 2. Personal information we may collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect:

- **Account data:** username, full name as provided, password (stored hashed), email address.
- **Optional contact:** mobile number in international format (if offered at registration or later).
- **Verification:** one-time codes, verification timestamps (for email or optional SMS/WhatsApp OTP).
- **Content you publish:** descriptions, approximate locations/coordinates you place on maps, photographs, timestamps, breed/type selections.
- **Technical data:** IP address, browser type, device identifiers, logs, security events (typically via hosting or standard server logs).

We aim to collect **only what is reasonably necessary** for the Service.

---

## 3. How we use personal information

We use personal information to:

3.1 **Operate the Service**: create sessions, authenticate you, publish alerts you submit, display maps/feeds.

3.2 **Verify accounts**: send codes by email or (if configured) SMS/WhatsApp; prevent abusive sign-ups.

3.3 **Safety and integrity**: detect abuse, enforce Terms, troubleshoot, prevent fraud or attacks.

3.4 **Compliance**: comply with lawful requests from authorities where required.

We **do not sell** personal information.

---

## 4. Disclosure

We may share information with:

- **Service providers** who process data on our instructions (hosting, email delivery, transactional SMS/WhatsApp providers, databases). They must protect the data appropriately.

- **Authorities** where we are compelled by law.

- **Others** where you direct us or where reasonably necessary to protect vital interests (subject to legal limits).

**User-generated alerts** may be **visible to the public or logged-in users** depending on design—treat descriptions and approximate locations as **potentially visible**.

---

## 5. Overseas transfers

Processors may store or process information **outside New Zealand**. Where required, we will take steps aligned with NZ privacy obligations (such as safeguards in contracts or disclosures).

---

## 6. Storage and security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational safeguards appropriate to our size and risk—but **no internet transmission or storage is 100% secure**.

---

## 7. Retention

We retain personal information:

- **while your account is active** and for a reasonable period afterwards (backups, dispute resolution); and  
- longer if **required by law**.

You may ask us to delete or correct information; we may retain **minimal records** required by law or legitimate interests where permitted.

---

## 8. Your rights

Under the **Privacy Act 2020** you may have rights to **access** and **correct** personal information we hold.

**How to request access/correction:** use the published contact route on our website.

If we cannot resolve your concern, you may contact the **Office of the Privacy Commissioner (New Zealand)**.

---

## 9. Cookies and similar tech

If we introduce cookies or analytics, we will explain them (including essential vs optional cookies) **before or at the time they are deployed**, and rely on lawful bases.

---

## 10. Links to third-party sites

The Service may link externally. Their privacy practices are their own.

---

## 11. Children

If you are under **18**, use the Service only with a parent/guardian as described in our **Terms and Conditions**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

---

## 12. Changes

We may update this policy. Updates will note a new effective date. Material changes should be surfaced on the Service.

---

## 13. Operator contact

The **Operator** identity and contact detail should mirror what is published with the Terms and Conditions on the Service.
