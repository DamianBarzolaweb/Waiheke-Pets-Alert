1. Purpose

We explain how we collect, use, store, and share personal information when you use Waiheke Pets Alert.

2. Information we may collect

  • Account: username, name you provide, password (hashed), email.
  • Optional contact: mobile number (e.g. E.164) if you supply it.
  • Verification: one-time codes and related timestamps.
  • Content: alert text, photos, approximate map locations, times, breed/type fields.
  • Technical: IP address, browser, device data, server and security logs.

We aim to collect only what is reasonably necessary.

3. How we use it

  • Operate the Service (authentication, publishing alerts, maps, feeds).
  • Verify accounts and reduce abuse.
  • Maintain security and troubleshoot.
  • Comply with lawful requests.

We do not sell personal information.

4. Sharing

We may share data with processors (hosting, email, optional SMS/OTP, databases) under appropriate instructions; with authorities when required by law; or where you direct us or vital interests require (subject to legal limits).

Alerts you publish may be visible to other users or the public depending on product design—treat descriptions and map areas as potentially visible.

5. Overseas processing

Some providers may process data outside New Zealand. We use safeguards aligned with applicable obligations where required.

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards for our context; no online service is perfectly secure.

7. Retention

We keep data while needed for the Service, backup or dispute windows, and longer if the law requires. Deletion requests are handled subject to lawful retention.

8. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you may have rights to access and correct personal information. Contact us via the details published with the Terms. For unresolved complaints you may escalate to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner (New Zealand).

9. Cookies and analytics

If we add cookies or analytics, we describe them—including essential versus optional mechanisms—before relying on consent or other lawful bases where required.

10. Links

External sites linked from the Service have their own privacy practices.

11. Children

See the Terms: under-18 users should engage with parental/guardian oversight. We do not knowingly gather children’s personal information without suitable consent.
