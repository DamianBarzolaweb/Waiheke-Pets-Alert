Help for Waiheke pets & owners

Quick links and practical tips for microchips, vets, travel off-island, and who to call when a pet needs help.

Vets & after-hours

Save your clinic’s number before summer weekends and public holidays — ferry delays happen, and a printed list on the fridge helps housesitters.

  • Ask your clinic about emergency cover and the nearest mainland hospital if a ferry isn’t running.
  • Keep vaccination and microchip paperwork photos on your phone.

Ferry & travel

If you’re reuniting a pet across the water, check carrier rules, shade, and water for queue waits — stressed animals overheat quickly in summer.

Lost or sighted — what helps

Clear photos, collar colour, last known bay or track name, and a calm “don’t chase” note make island-wide shares far more effective than a vague description.
